List of Electrochemistry Books

Revision date: July 18, 2015.
Go to: Electrochemistry Knowledge, the home base, from where you can connect to ESTIR, Dictionary, and the Encyclopedia. Recent books – Year index – Search – ESTIR Home Page – Electrochemistry Encyclopedia – Electrochemistry Dictionary – ECS Home Page

This compilation contains books on electrochemistry: science, engineering, and technology published in English since 1950. Textbooks, handbooks, monographs, and advances type (multiauthor) books are included; however, proceedings of meetings, symposia, and workshops are not. The file contains more than 1,500 books.

The collection is complete till 2014. Further items will be added only after the Society appoints a new editor.

The books are listed in chronological order by year of publication, and in alphabetical order by first author for each year. The file can be viewed in its entirety or by year. If you wish, you can also carry out a keyword search of this file.

See also compilations of electrochemistry review chapters, proceedings, and books published before 1950.
