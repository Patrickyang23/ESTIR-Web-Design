Introduction

This site contains thousands of general and varied information items about electrochemistry, electrochemical engineering, and electrochemical technology in fourteen sections.

It is difficult not to miss some information (links, etc) in a compilation of this type, and it is difficult not to make occasional mistakes. Further items will be added only after the Society appoints a new editor.
