List of Electrochemistry Books Published Before 1950

Revision date: October 8, 2014.
Go to: Year index – Search – ESTIR Home Page – Electrochemistry Encyclopedia – Electrochemistry Dictionary – ECS Home Page

This compilation contains more than 500 books on electrochemistry: science, engineering, and technology published in English before 1950. Many of these are probably of historical interest only. The books are listed in chronological order by year of publication, and in alphabetical order by first author for each year.

Many of them are now available on the Internet. Links provided.

If you wish, you can also carry out a keyword search of this file

It is difficult not to miss some books in a compilation of this type, and it is difficult not to make occasional mistakes. Additions and corrections are welcome, send them to: nagyz@email.unc.edu.

See also electrochemistry compilations for the time period after 1950: books, review chapters, and proceedings.
